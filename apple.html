Apple Restaurant

Apple Restaurants is a food chain that was founded with the concept of serving global food at affordable prices housed in the air-conditioned comfort of shopping malls. With the opening of its’ first and largest yet flagship outlet at two floors ofSarathCity Capital Mall located at Kondapur, the restaurant chain brings together the world’s best fast food brands under a single roof. Promoting the appreciation of cultures and heritage in world cuisine, Apple Restaurant continues to hand-pick reputable, dedicated brands to reflect the authentic tastes that people love, while providing an immersive experience for all senses, which includes the ‘live’ cooking demonstration delivered through each stall’s open-kitchen concept.
